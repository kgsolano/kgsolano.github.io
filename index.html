About Me

I am a former teacher turned software engineer. My transition into software comes from the undeniable impact that tech has on our lives and I believe that my passion to leave an impact on others has the best chance as a software developer. To be able to find solutions to special problems is something that I have come to have a strong desire for.

My aptitude for problem solving, my desire to learn, and my adaptability has helped me learn Javascript, Python, React, Redux, Flask, Express, Node.js, SQLite, SQLAlchemy, and git. As a lifelong learner I have quickly fallen in love with the programming process and the endless opportunity to continue learning. To be able to have an open approach to problem solving is something I have always valued as a teacher, and now as a software engineer.

In my free time I love to enjoy being in the mountains camping, snowboarding, or hiking.
